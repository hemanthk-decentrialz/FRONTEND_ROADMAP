🧩 Semantic HTML

<article>

Self-contained content, like a blog post or news story.

<section>

Thematic grouping of content, usually with a heading.

<aside>

Sidebar or complementary information.

<nav>

Primary navigation block (used in the header).

<main>

Dominant content of the page (this whole section is inside <main>).

<footer>

Footer of the page or section (see page bottom).
